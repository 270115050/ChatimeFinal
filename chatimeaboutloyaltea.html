WHAT ARE THE BENEFITS OF CHATIME LOYALTY PROGRAM?


1. Earn points. Earn back 10% of all your purchases in points and use accumulated points to redeem drinks and food items.
Redemption is at 1 point = P1 value.
2. Exclusive Freebies and Discounts for Members only
3. Lifetime Membership, no need to renew or register again.

HOW DOES THE CHATIME LOYALTY PROGRAM WORK?

Q: How do I join?
A: Just register your mobile phone number for free on our Loyalty Program Tablet at any participating store and start earning points today. No need for membership cards, just use your mobile number to transact every time you visit Chatime.
Q: What are Chatime points? Do the points expire?
A: Chatime points are earned when you buy from Chatime. Use the points towards food items and drinks from Chatime. Accumulated Chatime points do not expire.
Q: How do I check my points?
A: Visit any participating Chatime store and enter your cellphone number in the Loyalty System Tablet to check.
